Spoon River Anthology Mention Network

How to Use: Below you will find the Mention-Network created from the poems of the Spoon River Anthology. You can navigate within the dynamic visualization to observe the graph with zoom capabilities. The nodes are selectable to highlight neighboring nodes. Using the search bar, you can search for the title of a poem and view its connections.
